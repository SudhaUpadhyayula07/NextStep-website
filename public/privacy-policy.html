Privacy Policy

NextStep is a student guidance project. This page explains what the app stores locally and how that information is used.

Information Used

The app may use account details, saved colleges, assessment answers, and chat messages to provide career, exam, scholarship, and college guidance.

Local Storage

In this version, user data is stored locally by the project backend for demo and development use. Do not enter sensitive personal information.

External Websites

College, scholarship, and exam links open official or external websites. Their privacy practices are controlled by those websites.
